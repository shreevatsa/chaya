About

The word chāyā is Sanskrit for "shade" or "shadow" (and also a kind of gloss giving the Sanskrit equivalent of Prakrit dialogue in plays). The files generated by this tool are intended to serve as a "companion" or "sidecar" to the scanned images in the PDF file.

See also: Ambuda: Proofing, Simon Willison's tools: OCR (starting point for this project), Scribe OCR (future inspiration?).
